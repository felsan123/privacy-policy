Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how LingoFresh (“the App”) collects, uses, and protects user and device data.

Information We Collect

1. Information Provided by the User

  • Username (nickname)

This information is used solely to create and manage user accounts within the App.

2. Device and Usage Information

The App may automatically collect the following information:

  • Device model and operating system version
  • Unique device identifiers (such as Advertising ID, where permitted)
  • In-app interactions
  • Crash reports and performance data

3. Automatically Collected Information

  • IP address (collected indirectly)
  • Cookies or similar technologies via third-party SDKs

How We Use the Information

  • Creating and managing user accounts
  • Providing and improving app functionality
  • Debugging, analytics, and performance monitoring
  • Preventing fraud, abuse, and misuse
  • Displaying advertisements in compliance with applicable policies

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase & Firestore
    Authentication, database services, analytics, and crash reporting (Firebase Crashlytics).
  • Google AdMob
    Displaying in-app advertisements.

These services may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell user data. User data is shared only:

  • With the service providers listed above, strictly to provide app functionality
  • When required by law or legal obligations

Data Storage and Security

User data is securely stored on Google Firebase servers. Reasonable technical and administrative measures are taken to protect data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for children under the age of 13.

  • Personal data is collected only when necessary for app functionality
  • The App complies with Google Play Families Policy and Children’s Advertising requirements
  • Parental consent is obtained where required by applicable laws

User Rights

Depending on applicable laws, users (or parents/guardians) have the right to:

  • Access personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of the user account and associated data

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and will take effect immediately.
